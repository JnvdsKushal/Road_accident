What is Trace?

Trace is an intelligent road safety platform that leverages artificial intelligence to identify high-risk accident zones across Telangana. We analyze real-time traffic data, weather patterns, and historical records to provide actionable insights.

Our mission is to empower authorities, drivers, and communities with data-driven tools to prevent accidents and save lives on the road.

Why Choose Trace?

Comprehensive AI-powered solutions for road accident prediction and prevention

Real-Time Risk Analysis

Advanced AI models continuously monitor traffic patterns, weather conditions, and road infrastructure to deliver instant risk assessments.

Interactive Risk Maps

Driver Behavior Insights

Predictive Analytics
